Featured Projects

Innovative engineering solutions and research work

Lightweight e-Axle Integration

Apr – Jul 2024

Engineered and validated a lightweight electric axle (e-axle) architecture for EV applications with focus on structural durability, torque density improvement, and packaging optimization. Multi-body kinematic studies, FEA, and topology optimization performed.

CATIA V5 SolidWorks ANSYS Workbench Topology Optimization
  • Complete e-axle housing geometry developed including motor interface, differential casing, lubrication channels, and mounting bosses.
  • Structural FEA for torque reaction loads, bending stiffness, bearing seat deformation, and load-path evaluation.
  • Topology optimization reduced ~18–25% non-load-bearing mass while maintaining strength and fatigue safety factors.
  • Thermal analysis verified operating temperature limits under continuous and peak loads.
  • NVH evaluation ensured resonance modes outside motor torque harmonics.
  • Optimized packaging for compact EV rear subframe integration ensuring manufacturability and assembly accessibility.

Modeling and Analysis of Vertical Axis Wind Turbine Blade

Apr – Jul 2024

Designed and analyzed a Savonius-type Vertical Axis Wind Turbine (VAWT) blade using CATIA V5, SolidWorks, QBlade, and ANSYS. Evaluated aerodynamic forces, structural deformation, and stress distribution under varying wind conditions. Simulations confirmed that aluminium alloy blades maintain low deformation and operate within safe stress limits.

CATIA V5 SolidWorks ANSYS QBlade
  • Developed complete 3D blade geometry focusing on aerodynamic efficiency.
  • Performed FEA to analyze stress, deformation, and structural safety factors.
  • Used QBlade for aerodynamic curve analysis and performance evaluation.
  • Explored composite and H-rotor (Darrieus-type) design alternatives.
  • Optimized blade design for improved performance and manufacturability.
